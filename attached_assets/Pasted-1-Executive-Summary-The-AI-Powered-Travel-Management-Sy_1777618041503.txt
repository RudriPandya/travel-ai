1. Executive Summary
The AI-Powered Travel Management System is a full-stack, intelligent platform that combines generative AI, machine learning, and real-time data to automate, personalize, and optimize every aspect of travel — from initial inspiration through booking, in-trip support, and post-trip expense reconciliation.
The system is designed to serve three primary audiences:

Individual travelers seeking intelligent, personalized trip planning
Corporate travel managers needing policy compliance, cost control, and reporting
Travel agencies that want a white-label platform to serve their clients

Key Design Principles

AI-First: Every user-facing feature has an intelligent layer — no dumb forms.
Real-Time: Live pricing, live alerts, live chat — stale data is not acceptable.
Policy-Aware: Corporate rules are enforced proactively, not reactively.
Offline-Capable: Mobile app works with cached itineraries when connectivity is lost.
Multi-Tenant: A single platform can serve multiple travel agencies under their own brand.

The heart of the platform. Users describe their dream trip in natural language and AI constructs a complete, optimized, bookable itinerary.

Free-text prompt input: "Plan a 10-day trip to Japan in April for 2 people, budget $3,000, love food and temples, avoid tourist traps"

LLM-powered day-by-day itinerary with:

Morning / afternoon / evening activity slots
Travel time estimates between locations
Meal recommendations (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
Local tips and hidden gems
Estimated cost per activity


Adjusts for: season, weather, local events, holidays, traveler preferences

Visual timeline editor — drag activities between days and time slots
Add / remove / swap activities, hotels, restaurants

Real-time collaborative editing (like Google Docs)

Flexible date grid: color-coded price calendar (green = cheap, red = expensive)
Filter by: airline, stops, duration, departure/arrival time, baggage included, refundability
"Nearby airport" suggestions (e.g., search NYC → shows JFK, EWR, LGA)
Seat map preview with cabin class comparison

Email + password with strong password policy
Social login: Google, Apple, Microsoft
Corporate SSO: SAML 2.0, OAuth 2.0, Azure AD, Okta, Google Workspace
Multi-Factor Authentication (TOTP app, SMS, email OTP)
Biometric login on mobile (Face ID / Touch ID)
Session management: concurrent session limits, forced logout, activity timeout

Role-Based Access Control with granular permissions
Resource-level permissions (user can view their own bookings only)
Corporate hierarchy enforcement (manager sees only their team's data)
Permission audit log

AES-256 encryption at rest
TLS 1.3 in transit
PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance for payment data
Passport / document data encrypted with separate key management (AWS KMS / HashiCorp Vault)
Data residency options: EU / US / APAC (for GDPR compliance)

PDPA compliance for Thai users, LGPD for Brazilian users
Cookie consent banner with granular controls
Privacy policy and terms versioning with acceptance tracking

Device fingerprinting and geo-anomaly detection
Manual review queue for suspicious transactions
Chargeback management workflow

WAF (Web Application Firewall) — Cloudflare / AWS WAF
